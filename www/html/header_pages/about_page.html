This website is a streamlined, minimalistic, and more modern data mining tool based off the Human Metabolome Database (HMDB). From the HMDB website, protein/enzyme source data was downloaded and parsed, uploaded to a MySQL database, and made easily accessible to users through the form of a search engine. The website was intended to provide quick, reliable data about proteins or enzymes found within the human body for metabolic, nutritional, or biochemical reasons.

Hello, my name is Dien "Ethan" Mach! I have chosen to create this data mining project about proteins and enzymes within the human body due to my educational background and hobbies. I studied biochemistry and medicine during my undergraduate career and pursued active hobbies like weightlifting, rock climbing, and tennis. As a result, I fell in love with human metabolism and nutrition. By creating this project, I wanted to hone my newly found bioinformatics skills in topics I thoroughly enjoy.
